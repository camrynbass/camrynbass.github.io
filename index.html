Seoul, South Korea. May 27th, 2026

I have been selected to present my small cell lung carcinoma research at the World Conference for Lung Cancer 2026 in Seoul!

Upcoming poster presentation and abstract publication in the Journal of Thoracic Oncology (JTO)

I am happy to announce that my abstract has been accepted by the International Association for the Study of Lung Cancer (IASLC) for a poster presentation at the World Conference for Lung Cancer (WCLC) 2026 in Seoul, Republic of Korea. I will present my research on small cell lung carcinoma (SCLC) on the morning of September 15th, 2026.

Abstract Title: Survival-informed transcriptomic classification of resected SCLC with improved prognostic and TME discrimination

Session: P3.13 - Small Cell Lung Cancer and Neuroendocrine Tumors Session Date & Time: Tuesday Sep 15, 2026 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM

I look forward to attending my first major international conference and learning about the newest global developments in pulmonary oncology research. I hope to meet many presenters and attendees, and I am excited to share my work with the world!

If you are a fellow WCLC 2026 attendee, please feel free to reach out and connect with me over email or via my LinkedIn page. See you in Seoul this September :)
